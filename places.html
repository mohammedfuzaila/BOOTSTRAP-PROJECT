BEST TOURISM PLACES IN INDIA


Ladakh

Ladakh is most famous for breathtaking landscapes, the crystal clear skies, the highest mountain passes, thrilling adventure activities, Buddhist Monasteries and festivals..

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra.

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its backwaters, beaches, tea plantations, and cultural heritage. It's also known as "God's Own Country".

Khajuraho

Khajuraho is famous for its Hindu and Jain temples, which are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The temples are known for their intricate carvings, sculptures, and architectural symbolism.

Varanasi

Varanasi is famous for its spiritual significance, ghats, trade, and handicrafts.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is home to numerous historical forts, palaces, and heritage sites, such as the Amber Fort, Mehrangarh Fort, and City Palace.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling is famous for its tea, scenic beauty, and cultural heritage.

Goa

Goa is a perfect blend of Portugal and Indian culture. People get attracted to this place to experience world-class music, dance, and sunset.

Manali

Manali is the town's of snow-capped peaks, pure rivers, deep woods, and lush green valley's combine to create a visual symphony that truly tantalises the senses

Nainital

Nainital is a famous tourist destination in Uttarakhand, India known for its lakes, scenic beauty, and temples.
